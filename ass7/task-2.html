Poori and Chicken Curry Recipe

Steps to Cook Poori

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, and water to make a soft dough for poori.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into small circles.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry each poori until golden brown.

step to cook chicken curry

  1. Clean and cut the chicken into pieces.
  2. Heat oil in another pan and saut√© onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Pour water or coconut milk and let it simmer until the chicken is cooked.
  5. Serve the hot poori with spicy chicken curry.

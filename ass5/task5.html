The 1969 Ford Mustang was launched in various trims but here we are going to discuss the most renowned model, the Boss 429. It is one of the rarest muscle cars to date and it was offered only in a fastback body style. The 7.0L V8 engine on this beast churned out 374.9 hp of max power and 610.12 Nm of max torque. It was mated to a 4-speed manual transmission that delivers power to the rear wheels. The 1969 Mustang Boss 429 was a high-performance model and its production was limited to only 859 units..
